Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

General Course Queries

Who is eligible for the course?

Students from diverse backgrounds, including non-tech (BSc, BBA, commerce, mechanical engineering, etc.), are eligible.

Is the course suitable for non-coding backgrounds?

Yes, both courses are suitable for coding and non-coding backgrounds, covering foundational to advanced topics.

What is the duration of the course?

The course duration is 9 months with evening and weekend classes.

How does the course benefit students in different domains?

Both the CSE and AI courses equip students with skills applicable across fields like biology, commerce, mechanical engineering, and GIS.

What is the course structure and content?

The CSE course includes programming, data structures, and algorithms, while the AI course covers machine learning and deep learning.

Programming and Classes Queries

What programming languages will be taught, and will R be covered?

Python is the primary language, and some R programming is included for data-related tasks.

Are classes live or recorded?

Both programs offer live classes with recorded sessions for flexibility.

What kind of projects will we work on?

CSE projects involve software development; AI projects focus on AI models and data applications.

Career and Placement Queries

Will the course help secure a good job or package?

Yes, both programs enhance job prospects, with AI targeting AI-driven industries and CSE providing skills for tech roles.

Is there a placement guarantee, and are there placement criteria like CGPA?

No placement guarantee, but maintaining a CGPA of 7.0 or higher is recommended.

Are internships provided?

Yes, both programs offer internship opportunities in relevant fields subject to availability and performance(AI and CSE).

What are the career prospects for students from non-tech backgrounds or those with career gaps?

Both programs help non-tech students and those with career gaps transition into tech roles.

Administrative and Fee-related Queries

What is the fee structure and deadline for payment?

The fee structure varies by program. Contact the respective team for details.

Can we pay fees via EMI?

Yes, EMI options are available for both programs, One EMI upfront and rest through a loan with the NBFC partners.

When does the course start, and when is the orientation?

Start dates for CSE is 4th November & 11 November for AI and offline orientation is scheduled in November end .

Platform and Access Issues

If they face any issue, can students reach out to the WhatsApp community admins?

Yes, students can contact WhatsApp community admins for assistance.

Miscellaneous

How will the programs help research students?

The programs offer research-based projects, faculty collaboration, and advanced tools for AI and computational research.

How can a working professional or a college student pursue this course with efficient time management?

Flexible evening/weekend classes, recorded sessions, and structured learning help balance study with work or academic commitments.

If my college exams collide with the course examinations, how can I manage with that?

There will be a flexible structure for exams on weekends with two date options available, allowing students to choose whichever suits them.
